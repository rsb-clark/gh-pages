The Roger & Sarah Bancroft Clark Charitable Trust

The Roger & Sarah Bancroft Clark Charitable Trust is a Grant Giving Charity set up by Roger & Sarah Bancroft Clark, a Quaker couple from Street, Somerset. It donates to Charitable Organisations including those of Quaker, Heritage, Relief and Education. The Trust concentrates on the West Country and the Grants are usually £500 or less. The Trust now incorporates the assets of the Hilda & Alice Clark Charitable Trust, named for two of Roger Clark’s sisters which had similar objects.
